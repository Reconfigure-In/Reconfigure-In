Careers at Reconfigure

At Reconfigure, we’re constantly seeking talented individuals to join our innovative team. If you’re passionate about AI, Machine Learning, and pioneering technology solutions, we’d love to hear from you. Explore our current openings and find out what makes working with us a unique opportunity.

Current Openings

Here’s a look at our current open roles:

  • AI Researcher: Lead groundbreaking research in artificial intelligence, developing new algorithms and models to advance the field.
  • Software Developer (Python/C): Design, build, and maintain high-performance software solutions using Python or C, contributing to AI and ML applications and tools.
  • Data Scientist: Analyze complex datasets to derive actionable insights and develop predictive models that inform business strategy and decisions.
  • Technical Writer: Produce clear, comprehensive documentation for our AI and ML products, translating complex technical concepts for diverse audiences.

Why Work With Us?

Joining Reconfigure means becoming part of a collaborative and forward-thinking environment. Here’s what you can expect:

  • Innovative Projects: Engage with cutting-edge AI and ML technologies that have a tangible impact on the industry.
  • Growth Opportunities: Benefit from continuous learning and career development in a rapidly evolving field.
  • Collaborative Culture: Be part of a team that values creativity, teamwork, and excellence.
  • Competitive Benefits: Enjoy a competitive salary and benefits package that supports both your professional and personal well-being.

How to Apply

If you’re excited about one of the roles listed and want to join our innovative team, please send your resume and a cover letter to ayush.th2002@gmail.com. Be sure to specify the role you’re applying for in the subject line of your email.

We look forward to receiving your application and exploring how you can contribute to our success!
